During my time at [Unholster](http://unholster.com), TI consulting form from Chile, I designed and implemented a full data pipeline that process real-time medical data from wearables, from extraction to insertion into a time series database. Also I researched the available tools in the market to design the Data Architecture.

Roughly speaking, to proccess the data I used Quix Streams and we inserted the data into InfluxDB

# Technologies

* Quix Streams
* InfluxDB
* Python
* Modal
* Terra
